Questions

Asked before you bought

Can it really go from freezer to oven?
Borosilicate glass is engineered for thermal swings that crack ordinary glass. Best practice: let a frozen container sit a few minutes before a hot oven, and never put glass under a direct grill or on a stovetop.
Are the lids oven safe?
No — remove the lid before the oven. The glass body is oven, microwave, freezer and dishwasher safe. The airtight lids are BPA-free and safe for fridge, freezer and transport.
Is 1040ml big enough for a meal?
Yes — 1040ml fits a full adult main: protein, carbs and veg. It's the size meal preppers actually use, not the snack-sized tubs most sets pad their count with.
Will the lids survive the dishwasher?
Yes — lids and glass are both dishwasher safe. Top rack recommended for the lids to keep seals in top condition longer.
