Can it really go from freezer to oven?
Borosilicate is built for the temperature swings that crack ordinary glass. As best practice, let a frozen container sit for a few minutes before a very hot oven. Never place it under a direct grill or on a stovetop flame.
Are the lids oven safe?
No. Remove the lid before the oven. The glass body is oven, microwave, freezer and dishwasher safe. The clip lids are BPA-free and safe for the fridge, freezer and transport.
Is 1040ml big enough for a full meal?
Yes. 1040ml comfortably holds a full adult main of protein, carbs and veg. It is the size meal preppers actually want, rather than the snack-sized tubs most sets use to pad their count.
Will the lids survive the dishwasher?
Yes. Both lids and glass are dishwasher safe. Putting the lids on the top rack keeps the seals in good condition for longer.
