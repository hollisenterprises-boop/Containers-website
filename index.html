Can it really go from freezer to oven?
Borosilicate glass is engineered for the thermal swings that crack ordinary glass. Best practice: let a frozen container sit a few minutes before going into a very hot oven. Never place glass under a direct grill or on a stovetop flame.
Are the lids oven safe?
No — remove the lid before the oven. The glass body is oven, microwave, freezer and dishwasher safe. The clip lids are BPA-free and safe for fridge, freezer and transport.
Is 1040ml big enough for a full meal?
Yes. 1040ml comfortably holds a full adult main: protein, carbs and veg. It’s the size meal preppers actually want — not the snack-sized tubs most sets pad their count with.
Will the lids survive the dishwasher?
Yes — both lids and glass are dishwasher safe. Top rack for the lids keeps the seals in better condition for longer.
