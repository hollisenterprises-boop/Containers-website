Questions

Asked before you bought

Can it really go from freezer to oven?
Borosilicate glass is engineered for thermal swings that crack ordinary glass. Best practice with any glassware still applies: let a frozen container sit a few minutes before it hits a hot oven, and don't put any glass under a direct grill or on a stovetop.
Are the lids oven safe?
No — remove the lid before the oven. The glass body is oven, microwave, freezer and dishwasher safe; the airtight lids are for storage, transport and the fridge/freezer, and are BPA-free.
Is 1040ml actually big enough for a meal?
Yes — 1040ml fits a full adult main: protein, carbs and veg. It's the size meal preppers actually use, not the snack-sized tubs most sets pad their pack count with.
Will the lids survive the dishwasher?
Yes — lids and glass are both dishwasher safe. Top rack is recommended for the lids to keep the seals in top condition for longer.
